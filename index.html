Welcome to A4I

A4I (AI Innovation & Inclusion Initiative) is a collaboration between Microsoft and IIIT Bangalore. Our mission is to leverage cutting-edge artificial intelligence to drive inclusion and sustainable impact for underserved communities worldwide. We aim to evolve, engineer, and deploy large-scale Digital Public Goods.

A4I is designed to nurture a strong ecosystem for AI-led innovation that prioritizes inclusive and sustainable development. With support and funding by Microsoft Philanthropies.

The home of A4I is Center for Accessibility in the global south (CAGS) at IIIT Bangalore.
