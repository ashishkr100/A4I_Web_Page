About A4I
AI Innovation and Inclusion Initiative (A4I) is a partnership hub between Microsoft and the International Institute of Information Technology, Bangalore (IIIT-B), collaborating with several non-profit domain partners. A4I's mission is to harness the power of Artificial Intelligence to solve real-world challenges in areas such as education, healthcare, accessibility, and agriculture. A4I will take up innovations developed and piloted by Microsoft to evolve and scale them in open source as DPGs (Digital Public Goods) deployable at a large scale. The broader goal is also to help foster a strong AI innovation ecosystem for effective social impact through the latest advances in AI technology.
