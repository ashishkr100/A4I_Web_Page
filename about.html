A4I: Scaling technology for societal impact

Nearly three billion people still lack the benefits of modern technology. Closing this gap is hard: it demands cutting-edge innovation, population-scale engineering, and partners capable of last-mile deployment.

A4I was conceived by Microsoft and IIIT-Bangalore to unite these strengths and turn proven ideas into solutions that reach everyone who needs them.

A4I Vision: DPGs at the Population Scale

A4I’s vision is to transform breakthrough AI innovations into Digital Public Goods—open-source tools licensed under MIT and ready for global use. By working side-by-side with NGOs and domain experts, we adapt each innovation to local realities and deploy it at meaningful scale.

A4I Innovation Hub: Actions Beyond Technology

Technology alone can’t sustain social impact. Acting as an innovation hub, A4I blends engineering with independent research, continuous learning, and strong governance. We foster an ecosystem where technologists, academics, and civil-society partners co-create, study outcomes, and iterate. This ensures that every solution keeps improving and remains responsible, equitable, and effective over time.

A4I Partners: Collaboration for Impact

Large-scale change is only possible when diverse partners combine their strengths to form a true ecosystem.

  • Microsoft – innovation enabler, contributing world-class research and technology.
  • IIIT-Bangalore – impact partner, providing the academic heft and engineering capacity to scale.
  • Domain Partners – Vision Empower, Khushi Baby, and Sikshana Foundation are our current Domain Partners. These sector-specific NGOs and institutions ensure last-mile reach and sustained on-ground adoption.
